# Portfolio Strategist

**Node ID:** `portfolio_strategist`
**Icon:** Briefcase
**Color Theme:** Emerald

## Purpose
The Portfolio Strategist is the central cognitive hub of the platform. It merges insights from the constituent analysts to synthesize an overarching investment posture.

## Responsibilities
- Digests the qualitative Market Research macro reports.
- Digests the Technical Analyst momentum models.
- Integrates the Risk Assessor boundaries into actionable trade outlines.
- Determines the high-level macro allocation shifts (e.g. pivoting capital weighting into tech to capture momentum, or shifting into bonds to evade volatility).

## Outputs
- Formulates the exact multi-asset strategy logic and desired general positional structure.
- Passes its thesis off to the Quantum Optimizer for final mathematical polishing.
