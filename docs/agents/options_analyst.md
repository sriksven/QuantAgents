# Options Analyst

**Node ID:** `options_analyst`
**Icon:** Activity
**Color Theme:** Amber

## Purpose
The Options Analyst evaluates the derivatives market connected to an equity asset. It focuses specifically on volatility mechanics rather than underlying equity value.

## Responsibilities
- Analyzes Implied Volatility (IV) and IV Rank.
- Evaluates the "Greeks" across strike prices (Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega).
- Scans options flow for unusual institutional call/put block activity using predictive metrics.
- Proposes derivative hedging spreads (e.g. Iron Condors, Covered Calls, or deep out-of-the-money puts) as defensive structures when requested by the Risk Assessor.

## Outputs
- Structuring of recommended option spreads to decrease trade capital requirements while maximizing asymmetric reward potentials.
