# Technical Analyst

**Node ID:** `technical_analyst`
**Icon:** LineChart
**Color Theme:** Purple

## Purpose
The Technical Analyst agent examines historical price and volume data to identify momentum and trend formation. It disregards fundamental business metrics entirely in favor of mathematical price-action models.

## Responsibilities
- Computes standard indicators across given time horizons (SMA, EMA, MACD, RSI, Bollinger Bands).
- Analyzes volume-weighted average prices (VWAP) patterns to ensure favorable institutional entry points.
- Identifies critical support and resistance zones based on historic chart configurations.
- Reports on current market geometry (e.g. consolidation, breakout, exhaustion).

## Outputs
- Technical risk rating (1 to 10 scale).
- Computed list of near-term price targets and technical stop-loss thresholds.
